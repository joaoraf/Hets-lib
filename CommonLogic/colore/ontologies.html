Ontologies

The ontologies in the repository are informally divided into the following clusters:
  • Foundational Ontologies

    These ontologies axiomatize the general mathematical structures that serve as the basis for the representation theorems of other ontologies in the repository.

  • Generic Ontologies

    The concepts axiomatized in these ontologies cover the range often associated with ``upper ontologies".

  • Domain Ontologies

    These are ontologies that extend one or more generic ontologies, and which are associated with particular application domains.

  • Incidence Structures
  • Linear and Partial Orderings
  • Lattices
